Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream

Description

Who doesn't love a cool, refreshing scoop of Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream on a hot summer day? Our homemade recipe takes this classic flavor to the next level with the perfect balance of fresh mint and rich chocolate chips. Creamy and decadent, this ice cream is the perfect treat for any occasion. Whether you're serving it up in a cone or enjoying a bowl at home, this recipe is sure to be a hit with everyone who tries it. Don't settle for store-bought ice cream when you can make this delicious Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream right at home!

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Fresh mint
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat milk and cream
  2. Add sugar and mint
  3. Let steep and cool
  4. Strain and chill
  5. Churn and add chocolate chips
