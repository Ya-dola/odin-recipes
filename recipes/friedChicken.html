Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

If you're a fan of crispy, flavorful fried chicken, then you won't want to miss out on this recipe! Our Crispy Fried Chicken is the perfect combination of juicy chicken and a crispy, golden-brown coating that will have your taste buds begging for more. We use a special blend of herbs and spices to give the chicken a delicious, savory flavor, and the cooking process ensures that each piece is perfectly cooked and crispy on the outside. Trust us, this is one recipe you won't want to miss!

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Brine chicken
  2. Coat with flour
  3. Dip in egg
  4. Cover with breadcrumbs
  5. Fry until crispy
