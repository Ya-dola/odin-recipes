Chicken Shawarma

Description

If you're looking for a flavorful and easy-to-make dinner option, our Chicken Shawarma recipe is the perfect choice! Tender strips of marinated chicken are grilled to perfection and served with warm pita bread, fresh veggies, and a creamy yogurt sauce for the ultimate Middle Eastern-inspired meal. The combination of spices and seasonings used in the marinade gives the chicken a mouthwatering flavor that's both tangy and savory. Plus, the recipe is simple enough to make on a weeknight, but impressive enough to serve to guests. Click now to get the recipe and start cooking up a delicious Chicken Shawarma dinner tonight!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Shawarma seasoning
  • Yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken
  2. Skewer chicken
  3. Grill or roast
  4. Shave meat off skewer
  5. Assemble wrap or plate with toppings
